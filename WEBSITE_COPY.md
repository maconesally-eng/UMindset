# COPY STYLE NOTES

## Voice Guidelines
- Direct and clear. No flowery language.
- Confident but not arrogant—prove competence through substance, not hype.
- Address the reader as "you"—make it personal.
- Use short sentences and paragraphs. White space is your friend.
- Motivational but not cheesy. No "unleash your potential" or "become unstoppable."
- Acknowledge skepticism rather than avoiding it.

## Formatting Guidelines
- Headlines should be punchy, benefits-focused, or pattern-interrupting.
- Use bullet points for scannable content.
- CTAs should be action-specific ("Book a Call" instead of "Learn More").
- Emphasize key points with bold text sparingly.
- Numbers and specifics add credibility (e.g., "90 days" vs. "our program").

## Placeholder Blocks Key
All sections marked `[REPLACE WITH...]` require client-provided content:
- Founder bio and photos
- Client testimonials and case studies
- Pricing information
- Location details
- Specific credentials and certifications
